Welcome to the Daily Calorie Intake Calculator by Rayleigh! Here you can the determine the estimate number of calories you can consume per day based off your gender, height, weight, and activity level. The goal of this calculator is help you consume the needed amount of calories in order to live a healthy life. This is also made to avoid the risks that come with consuming a low or high amount of calories per day.
